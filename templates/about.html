About

Simulate the Democratic Party 2020 primary elections here on PuppetPrimary.com by experimenting with state results and allocating delegates among presidential candidates on an interactive United States map.

How to use:

  1. Choose a Candidate or Custom Mode
    • 'Custom' is the default choice. It allows you to click a state and set customized results first and foremost.
    • Selecting a candidate allows you to click a state and conveniently set your candidate in the lead.
  2. Experiment with State Results
    • Manage and experiment with a state's statewide results through percentage points.
    • Unallocated and available percentage points are always displayed. Remove points from candidates to give yourself a basket of points to use.
    • Make use of the 'Custom' mode when diving deep into numerous state results. It helps avoid mistakenly overriding already modified states with a 'Candidate' click.
  3. Count the Delegates
    • Click 'Count Delegates' to calculate and display the share of delegates candidates have recieved exclusively from already customized state results (dyed states).
    • Hovering over a slice of the donut chart reveals a candidate's overall percentage of pledged delegates nationwide as well as their current delegate count.

Democaritc Primary/Caucus System Short Notes

  • A candidate needs a majority of pledged delegates going into the DNC to clinch the nomination on the first round.
  • PuppetPrimary.com counts pledged delegates. Pledged delegates are bound to presidential contenders based on state results going into the DNC. Unpledged delegates (superdelegates) are not bound to any candidate and are only allocated at the DNC in the event of a contested convention.
  • Pledged delegates at all levels are proportionally allocated using the same delegate allocation algorithm.
  • A 15% result threshold is required to recieve delegates at either the congressional or statewide level. While a varying portion of numerous states' delegates are pledged based on congressional district or caucus precinct results, PuppetPrimary.com calculates all delegates based on statewide results.
  • Currently, Primary-Map.com only provides options for the 50 US states but there are plans to add DC, Puerto Rico, the US territories, and Democrats abroad in the near future.
