About


Primary-map.com is an interactive primary election map for the Democratic Party 2020 primary elections.


How to use:

  1. Choose a Candidate
    • 'Custom' is the default choice. It allows you to click a state and set custom results first and foremost.
    • Selecting a candidate allows you to click a state and conveniently set your candidate in the lead.
  2. Experiment with State Results
    • Manage and experiment with a state's popular vote results in percentages.
    • Unallocated and available percentage points are displayed. Remove points from candidates to give yourself a basket of points to use.
  3. Count the Delegates
    • Click 'Count Delegates' to get the current pledged delegate count among the states with established results (colored).
    • The donut chart represents the proportion of the colored states
